About us

Welcome to Edulinkers, your trusted partner in shaping your international education dreams into a reality. Edulinkers is a part of the Sheba Group conglomerate which has been in the Tech business for almost two decades. Our website serves as an online platform for students seeking guidance and support in the process of applying for student visas to study abroad. We are a dynamic and forward-thinking student visa consultancy platform dedicated to simplifying the complex journey of studying abroad.

Why Edulinkers?

Starting with wide experienced & expert counseling team, University admission support, Visa assistance support, Education guidance and Career counselling, Education Loan support, IELTS preparation course & Mock test facilities, Premium venue for IELTS exam, PSW (post study work) & PR (permanent residence) guidance, Air ticket booking to even pre accommodation services in some countries- we are a one-stop solution!
